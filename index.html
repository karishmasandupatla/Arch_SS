About the Studio

Sanjana Sandupatla Architects is a Pune-based high-end luxury architecture and interior design studio delivering refined, functional, and timeless design solutions. Our work responds to site, lifestyle, and context while maintaining strong aesthetic clarity and technical discipline.

We believe that architecture should not only serve its purpose but also inspire and enhance the human experience. Each project is a unique opportunity to create premium luxury spaces that resonate with their occupants and surroundings.

Our Design Philosophy

Innovate. Connect. Create.

We design luxury spaces that are purposeful, human-centric, and enduring. Each project is approached with sensitivity towards context, materiality, and the evolving needs of high-end contemporary living.
